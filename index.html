🧠 The Challenge

Brain metastases affect 20-40% of advanced cancer patients. Current neoantigen prediction tools are:

⚠️

Inaccurate

High false-positive rates (20-30%) waste R&D resources and delay development.

🧬

Not Brain-Specific

Trained on peripheral cancers; fail to capture brain metastasis biology.

🌍

Biased

Eurocentric training data → poor performance for African and Asian populations.

🚀 Our Solution

AI-powered neoantigen prediction platform for pharma R&D

🤖

Ensemble AI Model

23-feature vector combining binding affinity, clonality, expression, and brain-specific markers. Outperforms NetMHCpan by 15-20% AUC.

⚖️

Built-in Equity Screening

CPV/pPVI metrics ensure unbiased target selection across all ancestral populations – addressing growing regulatory requirements.

🔄

Closed-Loop Learning

Validation data retrains models, continuously improving accuracy (+19% AUC over 4 sprints).
